Market Research and Analytics


Customers are the supreme! And their expectations of the firm will influence our future vision.


In this globalized world, with zillion varieties for a single product, a product/service which suits the customer requirements is crucial for a firm’s growth. Thus evolved the concept of Market Research.



Brand is the identity of the firm’s values and testimony of how a customer perceives the products/service provided. Building an aspiring brand requires sheer efforts accompanied by decisive managerial insights.



Market research helps the firm in,
  • ➤ Gap Analysis: Examining and assessing the current performance of the product/service to bridge the gap between where you are and where you’d like to be. By grasping the essence of your desired vision, we help pave the path for your success.
  • ➤ Industry Analysis: Exploring the various supports and constraints in the Macro environment of the Industry.
  • ➤ Market Definition: Defining the market size, and growth of the particular product by understanding the demand for a product/service.
  • ➤ Market Segmentation: Understand the segments of customers and their characteristics.
  • ➤ Target Market: Selecting a Target segment and positioning the product/service among the target segment.
Marx and Smith’s assists firms in creating a strong and sustainable brand, tailor-made to imbibe the founding values of the firm.



With our Milestone Based Approach (MBA) for brand building and market research, we provide marketing strategies and ideas with performance metrics to measure and optimize all marketing-related activities.
