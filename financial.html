Financial Modeling and Forecasting


Financial capability is the backbone for all activities in the firm. A stable and supportive financial structure helps the firm to plan and play its strategies for the future.



A startup or a medium-scale enterprise usually has a narrow range of avenues for cash generation. With a very dynamic industrial environment the raw materials and the prices of other required inputs changes the financial bottom line in an unprecedented fashion.



To improve the profitability and maintain the sustainability in operations, the first step is to have an agile financial model.


Financial Modeling helps the firm in,
  • ➤ Status Check: Understand the firm’s current and future financial position based on a defined set of management objectives.
  • ➤ Break-even and Profitability Analysis: Break-even and Profitability Analysis: Effect on Profitability after Incorporating the future changes in prices and other costs involved in offering the product and service.
  • ➤ Budget Optimization: Understand the firm’s expenses by analyzing past data and making sure that the expenditure stays within the planned budget.
  • ➤ Budget segmentation: Each project has an unique resource requirement, thus prioritizing and allocating the available resources optimally enables the firm to fulfill it’s overall objective.
  • ➤ Spend Analysis: This becomes a parallel outcome of a financial model, providing more transparency and accountability for the resources spent in the firm.

The Marx and Smith’s Financial Analysis team seconds the words of Benjamin Franklin, “Beware of little expenses; a small leak will sink a great ship.” The team helps organizations come up with a strong and agile financial model considering all the needs and requirements of the firm
