Operations Modelling


All the activities which bring the products/services to life come under the purview of Operations Management. Being one of the most important activities for building a sustainable business, this requires industry-specific skills and expertise for efficient management.




With the founding principles like Deming’s 14 points and Crosby’s 14 steps for improvement, it has been proved time and time again that there is always a space for optimization and improvement in an organization. This is more soundly pronounced with the technological advancements and changing consumer expectations.



Having a strong operations model for different projects in the organization, aids in being cost-efficient and to become a pioneer in its field.



Operations Modeling and Optimization solutions help the firm in,
  • ➤ Quality Function Deployment (QFD): Being described as the voice of the customer, the inputs from the marketing department have to be converted to technical specifications and based on the firm’s capabilities the feasibility check is done.
  • ➤ Flow and Bottleneck Analysis: Understand the required steps/activities to be done to complete a task and estimate the bottlenecks involved.
  • ➤ Efficiency Analysis: Often there is a lag between expected output and actual output. Operations modeling helps the firm to trace and track all the activities to identify the resource leakage and optimize it.
  • ➤ Contingency planning: Not all goes by plan, with a diverse supply chain spread across the globe, there should always be an alternative course of action incase things go south.
  • ➤ Agile Methodology: Processes are meant to be improved over time. As technologies evolve, the firm needs to adapt to the changing industrial scenario in order to have a competitive advantage. This also involves implementing TQM principles in the organization.

Marx and Smith’s Operations Analysis team believes that “In this cosmopolitan world, it’s not only the hard work that produces results. Its by creating an operational system which multiplies and maximizes the output”.

The team helps organizations come up with Industry-Leading business models that are sustainable, profitable, and practically agile.
